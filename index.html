Spring 2026 Groups

We meet roughly every 2-3 weeks on Saturday afternoons. We start with the more advanced groups at 3pm, with the earlier groups joining at 4:10pm (and the most advanced group transitioning to teaching for those interested).

Group A

MOEMS Div E

Accessible introduction to competition math with 5 problems per test.

Sat 4:10-6pm

Accessible introduction to competition math. 5 problems per test.

  • Prereq: Multi-digit mult/div, 4th grade math
  • Focus: Intro to problem solving

Group B

Early Mathcounts

Transition to more intense competitions with many more problems to review.

Sat 4:10-6pm

Transition to intense competitions (30 problems). Extended review time.

  • Prereq: Variables, simple equations, 6th grade math
  • Focus: Speed and accuracy

Group C

Late Mathcounts

Transition to harder problems that require full knowledge of Algebra I and Geometry.

Sat 3-6pm

Advanced middle school competition level. Harder problems.

  • Prereq: Systems of eq, 7th grade math
  • Focus: Advanced topics (quadratics, etc.)

Group D

Begin AMC 10

Transition to high school competitions starting similar to National Mathcounts level.

Sat 3-4pm

High school level. Optionally stay till 6pm to help teach Groups A & B.

  • Prereq: Geometry, high MOEMS/Mathcount scores
  • Focus: AMC 10/12, AIME qualification
